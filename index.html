Workouts for Molly

Started January 24, 2019

Workout 1

3 circuits of the following:

  • Plank
    (30 seconds)

    Notes: If you need to modify this at first, you can do it elbows and knees, but the goal is toes and straight arms

  • Squats
    (10 reps)

    Notes: Make sure your knees don't go further forward than your toes. This will keep your form good, and prevent you from hurting yourself.

  • Lateral Lunge
    (10 reps each side)

Workout 2

3 circuits of the following:

  • Knee Grab
    (to failure)

    Notes: STOP if you experience lower back pain. You can substitue this with reverse crunches.

  • Reverse Lunges
    (10 each side)

    Notes: Front leg goes only to 90 degrees, with the knee over your ankle.

  • Pushups
    (to failure)

    Notes: It ok to modify this, but if you're doing more than 20 per set, you need to increase difficulty

Workout 3

  • Yoga
    At least 20 min

    Notes: It's ok to start basic.
